Formula Help

  • Overall Quality: (Conformation + DisciplineStat + Rideability) / 3
  • DisciplineStat: Dressage=Movement, Showjumping=Scope, Eventing=(Movement+Scope)/2
  • Sale Value: (Quality × AgeBase + WinningsBonus) × ReputationMultiplier × MarketMultiplier
  • Reputation Multiplier: 1 + (Reputation / 150)
  • Competition Effect: yearly random winnings + rep changes for active horses
  • Veterinary Effect: checkup reveals injury, heal improves soundness, AI chance boosted by Breeding Wing
  • Facility Bonus: Breeding Wing + fertility, Vet Station - emergency/vet pressure, arena boosts training gains
