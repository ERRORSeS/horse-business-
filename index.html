🐴 Overall System Structure

Dark blue, rounded, clickable tab layout inspired by premium horse-management game dashboards.

🐎 Horses Tab (Core System)

Interacts with: Cash, Reputation, Horse Stats, Market Conditions, and Annual Events.

Sub-Tabs (Open/Close Lists)

🟢 Active Horses Age 3+, in training/competition, can earn money, gain reputation, and move to Sales/Breeding/Auctions.

🟡 Young Horses Age 1–3, development stage, can train, cannot officially compete, and can be sold as prospects.

🔵 Breeding Horses Retired broodmares, stallions, and retired competition horses generating foals/stud revenue.

Horse Data Template
  • Name, Age, Gender, Discipline, Status
  • All stats, Winnings, Health Status, Market Value
  • Assigned Staff, Fatigue Level

Buy horses from NPCs. Purchased horses enter the Active tab.

Base Price = (Conformation + Primary Stat + Bloodline) × Market Multiplier

🏷 Auctions Tab

  • List up to 30 horses
  • Fields: Starting Price, Reserve Price, Discipline Tag, Marketing Level

NPC bids are based on Horse Quality, Reputation, Market Condition, and Discipline Demand.

  • Bidding ends at no higher offers or reserve not met
  • Fees: 5–10% commission
  • Competition wins attract more bidders
  • Reputation > 400 can bring international buyers

🏥 Veterinary Tab (Deep System)

Services

  • Artificial Insemination (mares)
  • Take Vials (stallions)
  • Live Insemination
  • Check-Up (all horses)
  • Heal Injury (injured horses only)

Injury System

  • Risk drivers: Soundness, competition frequency, fatigue, staff quality
  • Vet Station reduces recovery time and costs

Risk = Base Risk – Staff Bonus – Facility Bonus

🔁 System Interactions

  • Breeding Wing → increases foal quality
  • Vet Station → reduces risk
  • High Reputation → increases sale value
  • Staff Skill → increases stat growth
  • Elite Arena → boosts training success
  • International Sales → higher profits
